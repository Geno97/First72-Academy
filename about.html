About us

Preparedness
is all we do.

First 72 exists to make readiness practical. We turn hard-won emergency-management know-how into training that helps everyday people act with confidence when it matters most.

Our mission

The first 72 hours decide everything.

In almost any emergency, outside help takes time to arrive. What happens in the first 72 hours — at home, at work, and across a community — often makes the biggest difference to how people come through it.

That's the gap we fill. Our courses are built to leave people genuinely ready to act: calm, capable, and clear on what to do — not just aware that they should have a plan.

From households building their first grab bag to councils coordinating a full civil-defence response, we meet people wherever they are on the path to readiness.

What we stand for

Our approach

Practical first

Every course teaches something you can act on. No filler, no fear-mongering — just skills that work under pressure.

Calm & confident

Preparedness isn't about panic. It's about quiet confidence that you know what to do when others don't.

For everyone
